About Us

KoolHomes .

is a revolutionary IoT (Internet of Things) based home and building automation solution designed to simplify and secure your surroundings. The Y5home suite of products is offered by Y5home Technologies LLP, a Gandhinagar, Gujarat based IoT firm under its flagship brand and since inception in 2013, Y5home has dedicated itself to provide affordable, next generation, easy to use IoT home and building automation solutions through technological innovation and domain expertise. Our core objective is to offer affordable, high-quality solutions to remotely controlled, monitor, secure and manage all your electronic appliances locally and remotely with minimal effort. Y5home is led by a team of highly qualified and integrated professionals with a deep passion for technology and a strong belief in integrating technology into human activities to increase convenience and minimize human effort..
